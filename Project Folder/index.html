About Our University

Chandigarh University (CU) is a leading Indian institution recognized for its academic excellence and global outlook. Established in 2012, the university is spread over 150 acres of lush green campus in Gharuan, Mohali. CU offers a dynamic learning ecosystem with industry-driven curricula, world-class faculty, and cutting-edge research facilities.With students from all states and over 50 countries, CU fosters a vibrant multicultural environment. The university is known for its focus on Science, Technology, Engineering, Pharmacy, and Management studies.
